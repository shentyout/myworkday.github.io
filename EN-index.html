My role in the team.

In my project, the team consists of a UI/UX designer (me), a front-end engineer, and a back-end engineer. I am responsible for conducting user research, designing workflows, interfaces.
Before project execution, the team's technical core will confirm the product positioning, direction, and technical limitations.
First, I conduct user interviews to understand user demographics, age, lifestyle, etc. Then, I categorize the required features and analyze the competitors in the market through exhibitions and forums to understand the target consumer group. I also download or purchase competitor products for team members to try out.
While analyzing our competitors, I will also create a preliminary user flow and simulation, gather feedback from the team on the testing, and start defining the system's operational flow and interface design.
During the system development phase, I worked with the front-end engineer to confirm the specifications of the objects and provided design drawings for their display on various devices. I communicated with the back-end engineer to classify and build data, while also verifying the system's smooth operation and functionality with users.
After optimizing the usage process, we made modifications and wrote change items for the engineers to review and modify. We also established a systematic prototype to prepare for future projects.
