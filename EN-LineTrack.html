How to integrate production systems and create a user-friendly digital line？

How can we integrate existing habits with an ERP system that's been used for over 10 years and improve system management efficiency?

ERP systems on the market are too large and lack flexibility.
The current ERP can't track production line status. Since the factory is small with different machine brands, full IoT isn't feasible. We worked with machine and ERP vendors to integrate data via API, creating a system that helps managers monitor machine status and defect rates in real time.
