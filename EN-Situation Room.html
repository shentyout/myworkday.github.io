Transparent and real-time production line

Before real-time visualization, managing the production line was difficult, with issues like inaccurate machine capacity, wasted energy, and poor manpower allocation. After implementing the Two-Shoulder AI system, information became transparent and immediate. Operators can be reassigned quickly, machine statuses are tracked, and inventory, quality control, and material adjustments are optimized.

We are currently working with machine vendors to optimize the accuracy of feedback signals, aiming for full automated monitoring of machine status, eliminating human error to make information more objective and accurate.
