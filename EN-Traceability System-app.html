Watch fish grow!

The goal of the production and sales digital record is to let consumers track where fresh products come from and show farmers and fishermen how well their products perform in terms of quality.

We cooperate with the Council of Agriculture to develop a digital aquaculture system for fish farmers.Fishermen record feed, fertilizers, and medication data. Consumers can scan a QR code for product information, ensuring confidence in their purchase and promoting local farming.

Our partners

We have an excellent product manager, a imaginative UI/UX designer, a humorous app engineer, and a logical and meticulous back-end engineer. As a UI/UX designer, I'm responsible for conducting user interviews, designing user flows, creating interfaces, conducting usability testing, and gathering feedback.

APP Usage Items and Limitations

Our digital record is a private platform and may not have high credibility. However, we aim to make it more comprehensive, efficient, and effective in managing aquaculture. We plan to build an agricultural cloud and connect with government platforms in the future to enhance our inspection and certification credibility.

Currently, the government has a resume platform, but most farmers find it too complicated and not user-friendly. They often get lost while using it, and the information presented to consumers is incomplete. The cumbersome process and unclear system information are particularly frustrating for older users. They need to hire "specialists" to input data, which increases labor and time costs.

User interview

Product traceability is a credible government platform for consumers to track fresh produce sources. With QR codes, users can access information. But how many actually read it? Our interviews included consumers and producers.

Consumer interviews
We interviewed 30 people: 26 females, 4 males, aged 25 to 42. Only 2 males scanned QR codes, both from tech and transport fields. We're focusing on females as main users due to their role in grocery shopping. Most shoppers rush through, focusing on freshness and price. They used our traceability platform for basic info, but lacked patience for full details.

Aquaculture producer interviews
One male interviewee, an experienced aquaculturist and top young farmer!
He finds the current process of inputting data before selling too complex. He needs to hire additional personnel for assistance, but there can be discrepancies in information transmission. The system's purpose is for fishermen to record the aquaculture process, allowing future consumers to trace it. This simplifies the process and prevents information transmission errors.

Safe and Caring Food

With detailed records for how things are made and sold, we can use this technology in different ways. Like in the pet business – people now really care about what their pets eat. Brands like "Lady flavor" and "Petstimes" are popular with young folks. Using videos online is a great way to start a business.So, our records need to change. We want more people to share with their families, making the info more fun and young!

Our system is private and currently not recognized by government authorities. Users are unhappy with the government's platform – it's not user-friendly, and quality control lacks third-party supervision.. Can consumers trust the food they have? Of course! We also cannot guarantee quality completely. To implement the entire production process, we need to digitize and transparently share resources, including hatchery inspection, feed types, drug items, and vendor information. Each step needs strict quality control and always updated inspection information. This requires a lot of manpower and resources, and it's hard for the government to do it at the moment. Our goal is to improve farming management by turning the process into digital information and uploading it to agricultural clouds. This helps reduce problems with controlling things in the middle. We want to make agriculture smarter and more standardized, decreasing human mistakes and making food safety even better!
